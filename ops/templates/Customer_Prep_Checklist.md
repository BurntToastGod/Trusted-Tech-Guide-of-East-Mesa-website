# Before Your Appointment (Quick Checklist)

To make the visit faster and easier, please do what you can:

- [ ] Have your phone/tablet/computer charged (or bring the charger).
- [ ] Bring any device passwords you know (do not text passwords).
- [ ] If we’re working on email, be ready to access your email account.
- [ ] If we’re working on streaming TV, have your app logins available (Netflix, Hulu, etc.).
- [ ] If we’re working on a printer, have the printer turned on and paper loaded.
- [ ] If you have pop-ups or scam messages, don’t delete them—we can review them together.
- [ ] Write down your top 1–2 goals for the visit.

If meeting in public:
- Choose a quiet place with a table and Wi‑Fi (coffee shop, community center, etc.).
